Privacy Policy for Stem Blast

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Stem Blast ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our educational game, Stem Blast.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of information necessary to provide and improve our service:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google to create and manage your account. We do not store your password.
  • Gameplay Data: We save your progress (Grade, Subject, Difficulty, Score) in your browser's local storage to allow you to resume playing. This data is not sent to our servers.
  • Payment Information: If you subscribe to a Premium plan, our payment processor, Stripe, will handle your payment information. We do not see or store your credit card details.

3. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Operate and maintain the game.
  • Personalize your experience by saving your progress.
  • Provide customer support.

4. Advertisements

The free version of our app uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. We have configured our ad settings to be suitable for children and to comply with Google's Families policies. These ads are not behaviorally targeted.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for users of all ages, including children under 13. We are compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what is necessary for the app to function (i.e., Google account info for login).
